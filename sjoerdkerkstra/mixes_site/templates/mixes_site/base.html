MIXES
by Sjoerd Kerkstra

Some of the best electronic tracks I've come across. Some of it is pretty fast. Some of it is pretty out there. All of it makes your life better.

Drama, no holds barred. Finality. Drinking too much, smoking too much, shaking your fist at the heavens. Everyone dies, everything fades. But before then, there's so much beauty yet to experience!

Dusty dirty tracks to polish your soul. The tracks themselves get more shiny as the mix goes on. Out of the swamp grass, into the city. Special thanks to dances with wolves on East village radio for digging up tracks 2, 3 and 6. Where do they get that stuff from?
